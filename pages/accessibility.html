Accessibility Statement

We aim to make SPLAT City accessible to all users. Current efforts include:

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper headings and landmark regions.
  • Text alternatives for images via alt attributes and figcaption.
  • Keyboard navigation support (skip link, focusable controls).
  • Responsive design for various screen sizes.

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please email access@splat.city with details. We will work to resolve issues promptly.
